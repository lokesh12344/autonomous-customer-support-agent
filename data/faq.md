# Customer Support FAQ

## Billing & Payments

### Q: How do I update my payment method?
A: You can update your payment method by logging into your account and navigating to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods. Click "Add Payment Method" and follow the prompts.

### Q: When will I be charged?
A: Billing occurs on the same day each month as your signup date. Annual subscriptions are billed once per year on your anniversary date.

### Q: How do I view my invoices?
A: All invoices are available in your account under Settings > Billing > Invoices. You can download PDF copies for your records.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and ACH bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

## Refunds & Cancellations

### Q: What is your refund policy?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, contact support within 30 days for a full refund. After 30 days, we prorate refunds based on remaining service time.

### Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel anytime from Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

### Q: How long do refunds take to process?
A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The time it takes to appear in your account depends on your bank or card issuer.

### Q: Can I get a refund for unused time?
A: Yes, we provide prorated refunds for annual subscriptions cancelled mid-term. Monthly subscriptions have access until period end.

## Account Management

### Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link.

### Q: Can I change my email address?
A: Yes, go to Settings > Account > Email Address. You'll need to verify the new email before the change takes effect.

### Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Contact our support team to request account deletion. We'll process your request within 5 business days. Note that this action is permanent.

### Q: Can I have multiple users on one account?
A: Yes! Our Team and Enterprise plans support multiple users. Add team members from Settings > Team > Invite Members.

## Shipping & Delivery

### Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and overnight options are available at checkout.

### Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to over 150 countries. International shipping times vary by location (typically 10-15 business days).

### Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track orders from your account dashboard.

### Q: What if my package is lost or damaged?
A: Contact us immediately if your package is lost or arrives damaged. We'll send a replacement at no charge.

## API & Integration

### Q: How do I get API access?
A: API access is included with Pro and Enterprise plans. Find your API key in Settings > Developers > API Keys.

### Q: What is the API rate limit?
A: Rate limits vary by plan:
- Pro: 10,000 requests/hour
- Enterprise: 100,000 requests/hour
- Custom: Negotiable for enterprise needs

### Q: Can I integrate with other tools?
A: Yes! We offer native integrations with Slack, Stripe, Salesforce, and more. View all integrations in our marketplace.

## Security & Privacy

### Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II certified.

### Q: Do you sell my data?
A: Never. We don't sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

### Q: Where is my data stored?
A: Data is stored in secure, encrypted data centers in the United States. Enterprise customers can request EU or Asia-Pacific hosting.

## Product Features

### Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
A: Yes! You can change plans anytime from Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades occur at the next billing cycle.

### Q: What's included in the free trial?
A: The 14-day free trial includes full access to all Pro features. No credit card required to start.

### Q: Do you offer student discounts?
A: Yes! Students get 50% off Pro plans with a valid .edu email address. Apply through Settings > Billing > Student Discount.
